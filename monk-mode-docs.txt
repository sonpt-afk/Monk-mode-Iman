# MONK MODE CHO WEB DEVELOPER: 6-MONTH TRANSFORMATION GUIDE

## Tổng Quan
Hệ thống Monk Mode 6 tháng được thiết kế đặc biệt cho Web Developer full-time, dựa trên nghiên cứu từ dopamine detox, deep work principles, và best practices của tech industry.

## CURRICULUM 6 THÁNG

### THÁNG 1: FOUNDATION & DISCIPLINE RESET
**Focus:** Dopamine detox và thiết lập thói quen cơ bản
**Target:** 2-3h deep work/ngày

- ✅ 1h screen time điện thoại tối đa
- ✅ 10 phút thiền định mỗi sáng
- ✅ Không YouTube/social media (chỉ work-related)

- Plan tuần vào Chủ nhật
- Review progress thứ 6
- Deep work block 2h x 5 ngày/tuần

- Tuần 4: Complete dopamine reset

## DAILY SCHEDULE CHO WEB DEVELOPER

### PRE-WORK (06:00 - 08:30)
- **06:00-06:15:** Wake up + Hydration (uống 500ml nước, không check phone)
- **06:15-06:25:** Meditation/Mindfulness (10 phút thiền hoặc breathing exercise)
- **06:25-07:15:** Exercise (Gym T3,5,7 hoặc Cardio T2,4,6. Chủ nhật rest/yoga)
- **07:15-07:45:** Shower + Breakfast (healthy breakfast, prep for work)
- **07:45-08:30:** Deep Work Block 1 (side project hoặc learning, phone airplane mode)

### WORK HOURS (09:00 - 17:30)
- **09:00-09:15:** Work Planning (review daily goals, prioritize tasks)
- **09:15-11:15:** Deep Work Block 2 (most complex coding tasks, no interruptions)
- **11:15-11:30:** Break + Movement (walk, stretch, hydrate)
- **11:30-12:30:** Meetings/Collaboration (standups, code reviews, team discussions)
- **12:30-13:30:** Lunch Break (healthy meal, walk outside, no work talk)
- **13:30-15:30:** Deep Work Block 3 (implementation, testing, documentation)
- **15:30-16:00:** Admin/Communication (emails, Slack, quick updates)
- **16:00-17:00:** Learning/Improvement (tech articles, courses, code refactoring)
- **17:00-17:30:** Work Shutdown (day review, tomorrow planning, close all apps)

### POST-WORK (18:00 - 22:00)
- **18:00-19:00:** Personal Time (commute, decompress, change mindset)
- **19:00-20:00:** Dinner + Social (healthy dinner, family/friends time)
- **20:00-21:00:** Personal Projects (side projects, learning, hobbies - optional)
- **21:00-21:30:** Reflection/Planning (journaling, next day prep, gratitude)
- **21:30-22:00:** Wind Down (reading, light stretching, no screens)

## SYSTEM CHỐNG DISTRACTION

### PHONE MANAGEMENT
**Morning:**
- Phone ở airplane mode until 8:30 AM
- No social media check trong 2h đầu
- Use physical alarm clock, không phone beside bed

**Work Hours:**
- Phone ở drawer hoặc khác room trong deep work
- Notifications OFF cho tất cả apps except calls
- Use website blockers: Cold Turkey, Freedom
- Check messages chỉ 2 times: 12:30 và 16:00

**Evening:**
- No phone 1h before sleep (21:00 onwards)
- Charge phone outside bedroom
- Use Do Not Disturb 21:00-07:00

### COMPUTER DISTRACTIONS
**Browser Control:**
- Block social media: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok
- Block news sites during work hours
- YouTube chỉ cho work-related content
- Use browser extensions: StayFocusd, BlockSite

**Communication Boundaries:**
- Slack/Teams: Check mỗi 2h, không real-time
- Email: Batch process 2 lần/ngày
- Turn off desktop notifications
- Use Focus modes trên MacOS/Windows

**Workspace Optimization:**
- 1 monitor setup (avoid multi-monitor distraction)
- Clean desktop, minimal icons
- Close unused applications
- Use full-screen mode cho deep work

## HEALTH CHECKLIST

### DAILY PHYSICAL HABITS
- **Hydration:** 2.5L water/day (passive tracking)
- **Posture Breaks:** Every 50 minutes (2 min stand/stretch)
- **Eye Rest:** 20-20-20 rule every 20 minutes
- **Exercise:** 30-45 min/day (morning workout preferred)

### NUTRITION OPTIMIZATION
- **Meal Prep:** Sunday batch cooking (2 hours)
- **Healthy Snacking:** Nuts, fruits, yogurt thay vì chips/candy
- **Caffeine Management:** Max 2 cups coffee before 2PM
- **Intermittent Fasting:** 16:8 window (optional: eat 12PM-8PM)

### SLEEP OPTIMIZATION
- **Schedule:** Sleep 10PM, Wake 6AM consistently
- **Blue Light:** No screens 1h before bed, use f.lux
- **Environment:** Cool (18-20°C), dark, quiet room

### WEEKLY EXERCISE SCHEDULE
- **Monday:** Gym - Upper body (45 min)
- **Tuesday:** Cardio - Running/Cycling (30 min)
- **Wednesday:** Gym - Lower body (45 min)
- **Thursday:** Cardio - HIIT/Swimming (30 min)
- **Friday:** Gym - Full body (45 min)
- **Saturday:** Active recovery - Yoga/Walk (30 min)
- **Sunday:** Rest or light activity

## SUCCESS TIPS

### Week 1-2: Foundation
- Focus on establishing wake-up time first
- Don't try to implement everything at once
- Track basic metrics: sleep, phone usage, exercise

### Week 3-4: Momentum
- Add deep work blocks gradually
- Optimize workspace for minimal distractions
- Start meal prep routine

### Month 2+: Optimization
- Fine-tune based on what works
- Add side projects when routine is solid
- Begin networking and career activities

### Long-term Sustainability
- Allow flexibility on weekends
- Plan for setbacks and recovery
- Regular monthly reviews and adjustments
- Celebrate small wins consistently

---

**Remember:** This is a marathon, not a sprint. Consistency beats perfection. Start with 2-3 habits and gradually build up to the full system over 6 months.
